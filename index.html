About

The Daily Mechanism

One mechanism a day. Every mechanism has a step-by-step breakdown, clinical connection, and memory hook — so you understand it, not just memorise it.

Three daily challenges

Each day brings three completely different mechanisms — one for each difficulty:

🟢 Easy — multiple choice, first step shown as clue.
🟡 Medium — type the answer, category/tag clue given, hints unlock after wrong guesses.
🔴 Hard — tags only, minimal hints, type the answer. Designed to stretch you.

All three modes give 3 attempts before the full answer and solution are revealed.

The content

Primarily pharmacology and biochemistry — the two subjects with the most direct clinical payoff. Chemistry (organic mechanisms, named reactions) is sprinkled in, especially at hard level. All content is written with medicine applicants and first-year medics in mind.
