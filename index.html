About

The Daily Mechanism

One mechanism a day — step-by-step, with clinical context and a memory hook. Built for medicine applicants and first-year medics.

Three daily challenges

🟢 Easy — read a clue, pick from 4 options.
🟡 Medium — type the mechanism name. Hints unlock after wrong guesses.
🔴 Hard — tags only. Drawn arrow-pushing diagram appears after your first wrong guess. 3 attempts across all modes, then the full solution is revealed.

Content

Mainly pharmacology and biochemistry. Organic chemistry (SN2, carbonyl reactions, benzene mechanisms, amide formation) sprinkled in at medium and hard level — all with clinical connections.
